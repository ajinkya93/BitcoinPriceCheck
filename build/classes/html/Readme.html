BiPC : Bitcoin Price Checker

  • This application displays the current value of Bitcoins in US Dollars (USD : $) and Indian Currency (INR: Rs.) and also the past variations in its value in intervals of 7 days from today.
  • The X-axis of the graph represents the dates from the current and the past 7 weeks whereas the Y-axes denote the Bitcoin values in US dollars and Indian rupees respectively.
    (Y-axis on the left-hand side : $, Y-axis on the right-hand side: Rs.)
  • To view the trends in Bitcoins' values in INR only, click on "Bitcoin Rates in USD", which is above the graph, so that it becomes striked out.
    Clicking on it again, will reverse the mentioned action.
  • To view the trends in Bitcoins' values in USD only, click on "Bitcoin Rates in INR", which is above the graph, so that it becomes striked out.
    Clicking on it again, will reverse the mentioned action.
  • Once, the rates in a particular currency have been disabled, the values on the opposite axis (left-axis after disabling USD and right-axis after disabling INR) are irrelevant.
  • Hovering the mouse over data points on the graph, will display the Bitcoins value on that particular day.
